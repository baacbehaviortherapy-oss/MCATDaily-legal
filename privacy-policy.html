Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04/09/2026  ·  Last Updated: 04/09/2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information MCAT Daily collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using MCAT Daily, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who We Are

MCAT Daily ("we," "us," or "our") is an educational mobile application designed to help pre-medical students prepare for the MCAT exam. The app is operated by Carlos Alfonso / MCATDaily Team, based in Miami, FL, USA.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: mcatdailyteam@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (used for account creation and authentication)
  • Password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication — we never see your plain-text password)
  • Phone number (collected optionally to enable the Friends Leaderboard feature, used only for friend matching within the app)
  • Display name or username (if provided)

Usage & Performance Data

  • Questions answered, subjects practiced, difficulty levels selected
  • Accuracy rates, points earned, and streak data per subject
  • Trophy and achievement progress
  • Daily spin wheel activity
  • Token balance and transaction history
  • Leaderboard standings (points and streaks)
  • Date and time of activity (used for daily reset logic and streak calculations)

Device & Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and error reports (used to improve app stability)

Purchase Information

  • If you make in-app purchases, Apple processes payment information directly. We receive only confirmation of the purchase and the associated token amount — we do not store your payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Deliver and personalize your daily question experience
  • Track your progress, points, streaks, and achievements
  • Display leaderboard rankings (your username and score may be visible to other users)
  • Match you with friends via phone number for the Friends Leaderboard feature
  • Manage your token balance, including purchases and spin wheel rewards
  • Apply daily question limits and midnight reset logic
  • Send account-related communications (e.g., password reset emails)
  • Improve app functionality, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Leaderboard & Public Visibility

MCAT Daily features a Global Leaderboard visible to all users, displaying the top 50 users by points and streak. Your username (or display name) and scores may be visible to other users of the app. We do not display your email address, phone number, or any other personal information on the leaderboard.

The Friends Leaderboard matches you with users whose phone numbers are in your contacts. We use phone numbers solely for this matching purpose and do not share them with third parties.

5. Tokens & In-App Purchases

MCAT Daily uses a virtual token system. Tokens can be earned through gameplay (daily spin wheel) or purchased via in-app purchases processed by Apple. Tokens have no real-world monetary value and cannot be transferred, sold, or exchanged outside of the app. Token balances are stored in your account and managed through our Firebase Firestore database.

All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple and is governed by Apple's own privacy policy and terms. We do not collect, store, or have access to your financial payment information.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore. Firebase is a cloud platform operated by Google LLC and provides industry-standard security measures. Data may be stored on servers located in the United States or other countries where Google operates data centers.

We also use AsyncStorage on your device to cache certain data locally for performance optimization. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted elsewhere beyond what is synced to Firebase.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with the following parties only as necessary:

  • Google / Firebase — for authentication, database storage, and app infrastructure
  • Apple — for in-app purchase processing and app distribution
  • Law enforcement or legal process — if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users

We do not share your personal information with advertisers. MCAT Daily does not display third-party advertisements.

8. Children's Privacy

MCAT Daily is intended for users who are 17 years of age or older, consistent with the typical age of MCAT test-takers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access — the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — the right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — the right to request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out — you may disable the Friends Leaderboard feature at any time by not providing your phone number or removing it from your profile

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mcatdailyteam@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

10. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us at mcatdailyteam@gmail.com. Upon deletion, we will remove your personal information from our active systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Note that anonymized or aggregated data may be retained.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of MCAT Daily after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
